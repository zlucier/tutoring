About me

Hello! I'm Zach. I am currently a student at McMaster University seeking an undergraduate degree in Mathematics and Statistics. I am in my fourth year and have seen many different areas and applications of mathematics during my studies. I cover all areas of math at the high school level, including IB Diploma Programme courses.

My teaching philosophy

I am a firm believer that anyone can be great at math. Many of the struggles people experience with the subject come from forgotten knowledge from previous years since the nature of math is cumulative and sequential at the high school level. It is my mission to close these gaps and to make definitions, results, and methods easier to remember by motivating them.
